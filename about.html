About Me

passionate about sport and education

I am a qualified PE teacher and sports coach, with over 15 years of experience. I specialise in EYFS, pre-primary and primary age groups, having regularly taught nursery children from the age of 2 years old. I have worked in a number of different schools across my teaching career, having held the positions of Head of Games and PE and Director of Sport.

Having grown up with sport surrounding me, my passion was ignited from a very early age and I went on to become an international netball player, representing Scotland from the age of 15. Sport has brought so much to my life and I am passionate about passing my experience on to future generations.
